Projects

A list of my data analysis projects, including data analysis, visualization, machine learning, NLP and so on

January, 2022
How Tweets' Contents Influence Accounts’ Attractiveness and Network Position
  • I-Hsuan Lee, Yu-Chi Hsu
  • NLP, Social Network Analysis

The study proposed a measurement for the uniqueness of text contents, and the result shows that the uniqueness and humor of posts affect users’ attractiveness, which facilitates human understanding of both text linguistic properties and factors that enable a powerful network position.

June, 2021
Predict the waiting time of outpatients
  • I-Hsuan Lee, Chih-Tien Cheng, Chih-hsin Li
  • SGD, Random Forest

Based on 10-month data from Taiwan Changhua Hospital, the study used the stochastic gradient descent (SGD) method and the random forest algorithm to predict the length of consultation and waiting time, dynamic modulating the prediction, helping reduce the waiting time of patients.

January, 2021
Air quality analysis and the impact of PM2.5/PM10 on health
  • I-Hsuan Lee, I-Ting Hsiao, Hsin-Ying Wu, Yung-En Chen
  • Tableau, LSTM

The project aims to discuss the present air quality situation and the factors that will impact PM2.5/PM10, using the results obtained to predict PM2.5 accurately. Besides, we also discuss whether the Google Trend keywords of certain diseases are related to air quality.

January, 2022
The Customer Credit Default Risk Prediction
  • I-Hsuan Lee
  • Logistic Regression, LightGBM


Due to the credit card crisis in Taiwan and the rising demand for faster approval loans, the project aims to use some personal features with several models to predict a person's potential default risk.

June, 2021
Game Theory on Market with the pandemic era
  • I-Hsuan Lee, Hsin-ying Lien, Chiung-Chih Chang
  • Game Theory

Since covid-19 has greatly affected our lives, we tried to explore what kind of game exists in the market during the pandemic, and discussed aspects of commodity prices, delivery services and logistics transportation.

June, 2020
The impact of Brexit on the UK economy and Asian Market
  • I-Hsuan Lee, Ming-Tzu Ho, Wing-Si, Wong
  • Stata, Regression

In the report, we discussed the impact of Brexit on the UK economy and some Asian index countries, which can help us to understand the relationships between Asia and the UK, speculating on possible development trends.
